Who we are

Dealing with more than 10,000 patients and answering over 20,000 calls for help every year, our small but committed team are dedicated to saving UK wildlife.

What we do

We specialise in the rescue, rehabilitaion and release of injured, orphaned or sick wild animal and will do everything possible to give them another chance back in the wild. No animal is ever turned away and the team will do everything they can to give any animal the best chance at recovery.
